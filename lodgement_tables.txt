lodgement_table_parameters

a lodgement object can have minimum of 1 and up to 12 files from the file_metadata_object list associated with it 
the lodgement_objects table is the link between the lodgement and the file 
beacuse of above lodgement:submitted_at and file_metadata_object:file_created_at timestamps need be within a margin of eachother 
each lodgement object will have one lodgement_validation 
processing_engine distrobution "PE-1" approx 30%, "PE-2" approx 50%, "PE-3" approx 20% 
ingress_location distrobution "UI-1" approx 10%, "UI-2" approx 60%, "API-1" approx 30%
assessment_outcome distrobution "PASSED" approx 60%, "INVESTIGATE" approx 10%, "REJECTED" approx 30%
lodgement_status distrobution "DRAFT" approx 10%, "TERMINATED" approx 10%, "LODGED" approx 80%
